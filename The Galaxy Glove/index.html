Mission Statement

Galaxy Gloves is dedicated to developing advancements in astronaut health monitoring technology. Our innovative wearable system integrates sensors to monitor vital health aspects including pulse, temperature, humidity, and radiation exposure. Through our web interface accessible to mission control on Earth and a real-time display screen accessible to the user, there is instant access to their health data. At Galaxy Gloves, we are committed to enhancing astronaut safety and performance through revolutionary health monitoring solutions.
